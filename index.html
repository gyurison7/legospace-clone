How "classic space" constructors have changed

  • «Starfleet Voyager» 1981

    The windows of the ships began to be painted blue and the hulls were decorated with white details.

  • «Surface Explorer» 1982

    All-terrain vehicles became more massive and powerful, and for the first time suspension was used for them.

  • «Galaxy Commander» 1983

    White has almost completely supplanted gray in starship design.

  • «Intergalactic Command Base» 1984

    At the time, the largest space set with 312 parts.

  • «Solar Power Transporter» 1984

    The astronauts are assisted by droids, and the first walker is brought to the planet's surface.
